Todo by email - Privacy Policy

  • Todo by email requires you to connect to Facebook.
  • Your Facebook Id is then used to be able to identify your items in the Cloud. No other information is obtained from Facebook.
  • The Cloud in this case is Amazon Web Services.
  • In order to send push notification to your devices (if you choose) the Device Id & Name of your synchronzied devices are stored.
  • In order to allow you to send email items to the cloud Allowed sender email addresses that you supply are also stored in the Cloud.
  • The text of the Todo items is stored encrypted on both the Cloud & devices.
